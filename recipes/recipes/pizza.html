Watermelon Pizza

Description

A slice of watermelon cut into wedges is the 'crust' of this summery pizza.
Top with fresh fruits and veggies for refreshing flavor and crisp texture

Ingredients

  • 1 center-cut slice watermelon, about 2 inches thick
  • seedless red grapes, halved
  • cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
  • cherries, pitted and halved
  • crumbled feta cheese
  • chopped fresh mint

Steps

  1. Arrange watermelon on large plate or cutting board.
    Sprinkle with desired toppings. Cut into wedges to serve
